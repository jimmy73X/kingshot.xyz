Game Features

Kingshot combines strategic tower defense with kingdom building in a medieval fantasy world ravaged by rebellion and war. Take command as governor and lead your people to glory.

Strategic Tower Defense

Deploy defense towers and units like Barbarians and Marksmen to protect your settlement from waves of enemy attacks.

Kingdom Building

Construct and upgrade essential buildings such as Town Center, Quarry, and Hero Hall to expand your kingdom and enhance resource production.

Hero Collection

Recruit powerful heroes of varying rarities through the Hero Hall gacha system, each with unique abilities to strengthen your forces.

Resource Management

Assign residents to buildings and manage resources efficiently to ensure your kingdom's growth and prosperity.

Alliance System

Join forces with other players for protection, buffs, and collaborative conquests against common enemies.

Diverse Game Modes

Engage in tower-defense gameplay with manual character control or turn-based 'Suppress' mode with up to 6 heroes against enemy stages.

Immersive Gameplay

Experience the depth and excitement of Kingshot's multifaceted gameplay, combining precision shooting, tactical warfare, and strategic resource management.

Tower Defense Mode

Manually control your character in a 3D environment to defend against waves of enemies. Position your defenses strategically, upgrade towers, and utilize your hero's special abilities to repel invasions and protect your kingdom.

  • Deploy defense towers at strategic locations
  • Command units like Barbarians and Marksmen
  • Calculate precision shots considering trajectory and distance

Suppress Mode

In this turn-based mode, deploy up to 6 heroes against multiple enemy stages. Strategically select your squad composition to counter enemy strengths and exploit their weaknesses for maximum effectiveness.

  • Strategic turn-based combat system
  • Create balanced teams of up to 6 heroes
  • Earn valuable hero shards and resources

Kingdom Management

Build and expand your settlement with strategic placement of buildings. Assign residents to optimize resource production, monitor their health and happiness, and establish laws to sustain your civilization.

  • Construct and upgrade vital buildings
  • Assign residents as workers, hunters, or chefs
  • Balance resources for sustainable growth

Winning Strategies

Master these key strategies to progress efficiently, build a strong kingdom, and dominate your rivals in Kingshot.

First 24 Hours

Your initial focus will set the foundation for your kingdom's growth. Complete these tasks during your first day to gain a crucial advantage:

1

Complete Chapter Missions

Focus on completing chapter missions quickly to unlock essential buildings and earn valuable resources.

2

Upgrade Town Center

Prioritize Town Center upgrades to unlock new structures and units that will expand your capabilities.

3

Join an Alliance

Find and join an active alliance early for protection buffs and collaborative advantages.

4

Participate in Intel Missions

Complete Intel Missions and Suppress battles to earn hero shards and progression resources.

Resource Management

Efficient resource allocation will accelerate your development. Follow these best practices to optimize your kingdom's economy:

1

Building Priority Order

Town Center → Training Camp → Research → Resource Buildings. Keep builders busy at all times.

2

Strategic Speed-up Usage

Save speed-ups for events and high-value upgrade timers to maximize efficiency.

3

Troop Training Sequence

Train Infantry first, then Archers, then Cavalry, and position Infantry in front during battles.

4

Resource Assignment

Assign residents to optimize resource production based on your current needs and development goals.

Hero Development

Your heroes are the backbone of your military might. Implement these strategies to build a formidable hero roster:

1

Recruitment Keys Strategy

Accumulate 10 Recruitment Hero Keys before using them to increase chances of getting complete heroes.

2

Balanced Team Composition

Create diverse teams with complementary abilities for different game modes and challenges.

3

Focus Hero Upgrades

Rather than spreading resources thin, concentrate on fully upgrading your core team of heroes first.

4

Complete Growth Missions

Prioritize growth missions that reward hero shards, especially for SR and higher rarity heroes.

Defense Optimization

A strong defense is as important as a powerful offense. Implement these defensive tactics to safeguard your kingdom:

1

Tower Placement

Position defense towers strategically to maximize coverage and create effective choke points.

2

Unit Diversity

Maintain a balanced mix of melee and ranged units to counter various enemy attack strategies.

3

Defense Technology

Prioritize defense-related research in your tech tree to strengthen walls and defensive structures.

4

Alliance Defense

Coordinate with alliance members to support each other during enemy attacks with reinforcements.

The Hero Hall

Recruit legendary champions to lead your forces to victory

The Hero Hall is Kingshot's gacha system where you can recruit powerful heroes to join your cause. Use Platinum and Gold keys to summon heroes of varying rarities, from Common to Legendary.

Advanced Recruitment

Uses Platinum keys with increased chances of Epic and Legendary heroes. A pity system rewards Mythical chests after many summons.

Epic Recruitment

Uses Gold keys with guaranteed Rare heroes and a chance to obtain Epic and higher rarities for your roster.

Pro Tip

Accumulate 10 Recruitment Hero Keys before using them to increase your chances of getting complete heroes, including Mythic ones.
